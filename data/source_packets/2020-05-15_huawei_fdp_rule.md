# Source Packet: Huawei Foreign-Direct-Product Rule

## Event Title

U.S. foreign-direct-product rule tightened against Huawei

## Event Date

2020-05-15

## Evidence Quality Assessment

High. The Commerce/BIS announcement and Federal Register rule provide official evidence for the date, actors, and regulatory mechanism. Event interpretation requires caution because TSMC announced its Arizona fab on the same date.

## Inclusion Rationale

The rule extended U.S. export-control reach to foreign-produced items using U.S. technology, directly affecting Huawei's access to advanced foundry output and making Taiwan-linked semiconductor manufacturing part of the U.S.-China technology control channel.

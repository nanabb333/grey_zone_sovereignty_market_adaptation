# Source Packet: TSMC Arizona Announcement

## Event Title

TSMC announces $12 billion Arizona fab

## Event Date

2020-05-15

## Source References

- TSMC press release, "TSMC Announces Intention to Build and Operate an Advanced Semiconductor Fab in the United States," issued 2020-05-15: https://pr.tsmc.com/english/news/2033

## Evidence Quality Assessment

High for event existence and date. The TSMC press release directly states the announcement date, planned Arizona location, 5-nanometer technology, and approximately US$12 billion spending from 2021 to 2029. Event-study interpretation is medium because the Huawei foreign-direct-product rule occurred on the same date and may confound market-window attribution.

## Event-Family Classification

`Strategic_Investment`

## Inclusion Rationale

The announcement is a major early signal of advanced semiconductor capacity diversification away from Taiwan and toward the United States. It is central to supply-chain security and strategic-technology policy.

## Theory Relevance

- `interpretation_type`: Opportunity
- `strategic_importance_level`: High
- `state_support_signal`: Medium

The event is coded as opportunity because it signals capacity resilience and strategic investment. State support is Medium because the release explicitly references mutual understanding and commitment to support from the U.S. federal government and Arizona, but this announcement predates later CHIPS Act subsidy terms.

# Candidate Variables

## Independent Variables

### Geopolitical Risk

Definition:
Political or military events related to Taiwan Strait tensions.

Examples:
- PLA exercises
- Pelosi visit
- Election events
- U.S.-Taiwan policy announcements

Measurement:
Event occurrence (0/1)

---

### Strategic Importance

Definition:
Events highlighting Taiwan's role in global semiconductor and AI supply chains.

Examples:
- TSMC expansion
- Nvidia investment
- AI factory announcements

Measurement:
Event occurrence (0/1)

---

## Dependent Variables

### Market Reaction

TWSE return

Measurement:

# Summary Results

The event-study results support a conditional interpretation of Taiwan-related geopolitical risk.

## Main Patterns

- Risk events are negative on average.
- Strategic-importance events are positive on average.
- Individual cases vary substantially across both TWSE and TSMC.
- Joint Sword-2024B is an important risk-event case because it generated positive abnormal returns in some windows.
- Market reactions appear to depend on investor interpretation, event repetition, strategic context, and surprise.

## Interpretation

The results do not support a simple automatic risk-damage model. Instead, they suggest that financial markets distinguish between different kinds of Taiwan-related shocks. Some events are interpreted as direct threats, while others reinforce Taiwan's strategic importance in global semiconductor supply chains.

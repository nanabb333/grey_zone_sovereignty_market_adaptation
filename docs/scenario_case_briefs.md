# Scenario Case Briefs

These briefs translate the scenario similarity results into analyst-support notes. They use historically comparable cases, contextual signals, and descriptive precedent. They are not predictive and do not provide investment guidance.

## Large-Scale Military Exercises Near Taiwan

This scenario represents a visible military-demonstration case involving exercises near Taiwan. The closest historical precedents are `Joint Sword 2023`, `Joint Sword-2024A`, and `Joint Sword-2024B`.

The linked evidence base includes placeholder-safe news rows connected to Joint Sword exercise coverage and related Taiwan Strait military activity. Historical descriptive market-reaction context is available through `tsmc_car_7` values for the matched cases.

Analysts should watch the scale, timing, geographic scope, official exercise framing, repetition relative to prior Joint Sword activity, and whether military activity is paired with diplomatic or economic measures.

Limitations: similarity reflects coded historical features and news metadata. It does not imply that another exercise would produce the same market reaction.

The closest historical precedents include `PLA Drills After Lai U.S. Transit`, `PLA Joint Combat Readiness Patrols Around Taiwan`, and `Joint Sword-2024A`.

Limitations: repeated grey-zone pressure may be less surprising at the event-family level, and similarity does not establish severity or future response.

## Repo 1: Grey-Zone Sovereignty and Market Adaptation

### Recruiter Experience

Score: 8/10

Recruiters can understand the project as a serious research portfolio, especially from the README, figures, and concise results. The topic is distinctive and signals domain expertise. It may be dense for non-specialists, but that is acceptable for an academic research repo.

### Analytics Hiring Manager Experience

Score: 8/10

The repo shows event-study logic, abnormal returns, CAR windows, coding transparency, and reproducibility. It is not primarily an engineering repo, but it demonstrates analytical maturity and careful interpretation.

### Master's Admissions Experience

Score: 9/10

This is the strongest audience fit. The repo shows research originality, theory-building, methodological caution, and strong topic relevance.

### Highest ROI Improvement

Add a short `data/README.md` explaining canonical datasets and archival/supporting files.

# Intelligence Product Positioning

## Product Logic

This repository began as an academic event-study project on Taiwan-related geopolitical risk and market adaptation. The current upgrade extends that research base into a dashboard-ready geopolitical intelligence prototype.

The product now has five connected layers:

| Layer | Role |
|---|---|
| Academic event-study research | Original empirical design, event coding, and historical market-reaction outputs |
| Curated news evidence database | Source-evidence enrichment linked to geopolitical events and event families |
| Event-family analytics | Descriptive statistics by geopolitical event type |
| Scenario similarity layer | Historical similarity analysis and context retrieval for example scenarios |
| Dashboard layer | Static decision-support presentation for analyst review |

## Target Users

The prototype is designed for:

- geopolitical risk analysts
- business analytics students
- strategy teams
- policy researchers
- portfolio reviewers evaluating research-to-product translation

## What The Prototype Demonstrates

The project demonstrates:

- data structuring across event, evidence, analytics, and dashboard outputs
- translation from academic research into a product-style intelligence workflow
- dashboard communication for non-technical review
- cautious analytical framing around uncertainty and historical context
- restraint: no overclaiming beyond the validated event-study design

## What It Does Not Do

The prototype does not provide:

- market prediction
- investment advice
- automated trading signals
- causal proof from descriptive similarity
- live intelligence collection
- automated LLM interpretation

All outputs should be treated as analyst-support materials that require review before publication or operational use.

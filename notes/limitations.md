# Limitations

This project is best understood as an exploratory research portfolio project based on a completed dissertation paper.

Important limitations include:

- The event sample is small.
- Event coding involves interpretive judgment.
- Some events overlap with global market movements or technology-sector shocks.
- Benchmark adjustment does not fully isolate causality.
- Short event windows may miss slower investor reassessment.
- The analysis focuses on market reactions, not the full political or security consequences of Taiwan-related shocks.

These limitations do not invalidate the project. They clarify that the main contribution is theoretical and interpretive: Taiwan-related geopolitical risk has conditional financial effects shaped by investor meaning-making and adaptation.

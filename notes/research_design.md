# Research Design

The project uses an exploratory event-study design to compare market reactions around Taiwan-related geopolitical and geoeconomic shocks from 2022 to 2025.

## Units of Analysis

The event is the main unit of analysis. Each event is assigned a date, name, and interpretive classification.

## Outcomes

- TWSE abnormal returns
- TSMC abnormal returns
- Cumulative abnormal returns over short event windows

## Benchmarks

- TWSE abnormal returns are benchmark-adjusted against NASDAQ.
- TSMC abnormal returns are benchmark-adjusted against the Philadelphia Semiconductor Index.

## Interpretation

The design is intended to identify patterns in market interpretation, not to make strong causal claims. Results are interpreted alongside event context, event repetition, strategic relevance, and surprise.
